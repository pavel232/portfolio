    • About me
    It’s all start with a question: «Why there are so many ugly and inconvenient apps on the Web?». And I decided to answer how they are created - from prototype to development. In 2017 I became familiar with the basic concepts of HTML/CSS and Javascript. In 2018 I joined EPAM’s internal Front-end Development Training, but my knowledge wasn’t enougth to start work in the company for that time. In September 2019 I joined The Rolling Scopes School and my knowledges get much stronger. But something was wrong. I wanted to be on the other side of the process and to take part in creating the app not only as a developer. I wanted to have an impact on the app/website concept. At the end of the course, I learned about the recruitment in DesignSpot School and I had a choice - to study the development or follow passion. I tried to combine, but after few weeks I realized that design is what really inspires me. I successfully finished first step of DesignSpot School and l’m not going to stop there. I continue to study theory and practice constantly, listen to podcasts and read all kinds of books about design. I’m happy that I do what I really love!
